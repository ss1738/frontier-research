# Consciousness & Cognition Science — Frontier Unsolved Problems

## TOP 3 GAPS

### 1. Intraoperative Awareness — No Reliable Clinical Biomarker
- 26,000 cases/year in the US of waking during anesthesia. BIS (1990s EEG algorithm) = current standard of care
- BIS paradox: monitoring sometimes INCREASES awareness incidence because thresholds are unreliable
- PCI (TMS/EEG perturbational complexity) = most accurate in research, but requires TMS hardware + 30+ second windows — not usable in OR
- **Specific gap**: continuous real-time scalp-EEG complexity measure working through OR electrical interference (cautery, pacemakers, muscle relaxants), distinguishing sedation from unconsciousness with <2% individual error rate (BIS: ~15%). No single feature invariant to anesthetic mechanism (propofol vs volatile vs ketamine) + individual neuroanatomy.
- **Company unlock**: next-generation intraoperative consciousness monitor. FDA 510(k). Revenue model: per-use device fees (BIS = $500M+/year for Medtronic). $5-10B market.

### 2. The Binding Problem — No Causal Test Has Been Run
- How does the brain bind separate features (color in V4, motion in MT, shape in IT) into a single unified percept?
- Gamma-synchrony hypothesis (40Hz binding code): dominant theory since 1990s but anomalies accumulating
- "Gamma oscillations biophysics precludes binding timing precision" (2016 PMC paper)
- **Specific gap**: no causal test run — focal millisecond-precise optogenetic disruption of gamma in single feature area (V4 for color) while measuring whether bound percept breaks apart. Feasible in NHP with current optogenetics. Has never been done, possibly because a negative result would invalidate the dominant framework with no replacement theory.

### 3. Substrate-Independent Consciousness Measurement
- 2024 NY Declaration: fish, cephalopods, crustaceans are sentient. Fungal networks show electrical oscillations analogous to neural spike trains.
- IIT phi (substrate-independent in theory): computationally intractable for >20 binary elements
- PCI: requires TMS + EEG — inapplicable to fungi, plants, distributed systems
- **Specific gap**: nobody has attempted perturbational complexity measurement in slime molds using electrical micro-stimulation + multi-electrode recording — the direct non-neural analog of PCI. Either result (high or low complexity) is the first data point in a substrate-independent consciousness scale.
- **Company unlock**: substrate-independent consciousness measurement platform. Customers: pharma (drug consciousness testing), animal welfare, AI consciousness assessment (legally relevant). $500M-2B research tools market that doesn't exist yet.

## WILD HYPOTHESIS
**CEMI (Conscious EM field) measurement**: EM field generated by coordinated cortical oscillations creates interference patterns at the field level not reducible to individual firing rates. McFadden proposed CEMI in 2002 but dismissed as unfalsifiable. The unfalsified experiment: 3D EM field reconstruction inside skull using high-density EEG + MEG + intracortical recording + computational reconstruction. Test whether field structure contains emergent information not present at any individual recording site. If yes: measurable consciousness in electromagnetic domain. A non-contact consciousness meter based on skull-external EM field measurement. No lab has attempted the reconstruction experiment.
