# Biotech & Gene Editing — Frontier Unsolved Problems

## TOP 3 GAPS

### 1. Extra-Hepatic In Vivo Delivery — The Non-Liver Wall
- LNPs deliver to liver at 90% efficiency. Everything else (muscle, CNS, lung, kidney, heart): collapses
- AAV: 4.7kb cargo limit (excludes prime editors), pre-existing immunity in 30-60% adults, no re-dosing
- Nobody has: programmable, organ-selective, re-administrable vehicle fitting >6kb payload with >30% editing in 3+ non-hepatic tissues in primates
- Blocks >80% of all monogenic disease targets (Duchenne, CF, cardiac channelopathies)
- **Company unlock**: new class of organ-selective gene editing vehicle — not AAV, not LNP, not standard exosomes

### 2. Mitochondrial Adenine Base Editing — 60% of mtDNA Variants Unreachable
- DdCBE enables C→T conversions in mtDNA. Adenine conversions (A→G, G→A) = most pathogenic variants = completely inaccessible
- The Broad Lab created DdCBE but no adenine deaminase works on dsDNA in mitochondrial environment
- Pretzel Therapeutics, ProQR, GenEdit — nobody attacking this specific gap
- **Company unlock**: mitochondrial ABE covering MELAS, MERRF, Leber's, Leigh syndrome. 1 in 5,000 people affected, zero disease-modifying therapy

### 3. In Vivo Boolean Logic Gene Circuits in Somatic Cells
- CAR-T logic works ex vivo. Building IF[antigen A] AND [antigen B] AND NOT [healthy marker C] THEN kill — inside a living body — is unsolved
- Cargo size limits for multi-gene constructs + no persistent expression without integration + no safe reversible on/off
- **Wild hypothesis**: Endogenous LINE-1 retrotransposons — most active in cancer/senescent cells — can be engineered as therapeutic delivery vehicles. Write therapeutic payloads into pathological cells using their own elevated reverse transcriptase. Nobody has attempted this.
- **Company unlock**: OS layer for somatic cells. First app: engineered beta-cell replacement for T1D. Oncology: $100B+
