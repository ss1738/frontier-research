# Energy & Clean Tech — Frontier Unsolved Problems

## TOP 3 GAPS

### 1. Solid-State Battery Dendrite Nucleation (UNKNOWN MECHANISM)
- Nobody has operando metrology imaging dendrite nucleation in real cells at commercial current densities
- Critical current density for dendrite formation is 3-5x BELOW what fast-charging requires
- Lab cells use 10-50µm electrolyte; commercial needs sub-10µm — grain boundary defects dominate, uncharacterizable at scale
- **Company unlock**: real-time in-situ nanoscale imaging of solid electrolyte interfaces during cycling

### 2. Grid Inertia Collapse Under 100% Renewables
- As solar/wind displace synchronous generators, grid frequency can deviate in under 500ms — faster than any protection relay responds
- Synthetic inertia exists in research but NO market mechanism prices it
- ROCOF (Rate of Change of Frequency) problem is completely unsolved at scale
- **Company unlock**: sub-second synthetic inertia market clearing platform — software sitting between grid operators and every inverter-based asset

### 3. Perovskite Outdoor Stability — the 25-Year Gap
- Silicon: 25+ year lifetime guaranteed. Best perovskite: 78% performance after 12 months
- Gap = 24x. Degradation is multi-physical (ion migration + moisture + thermal cycling + lead leaching) — no single fix
- IEC 61215 accelerated test protocols designed for silicon DON'T predict perovskite failure modes
- **Company unlock**: multi-mechanism passivation + new accelerated testing standard
