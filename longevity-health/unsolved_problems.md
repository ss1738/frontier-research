# Longevity & Aging — Frontier Unsolved Problems

## TOP 3 GAPS

### 1. Tissue-Specific Aging — No Targeted Intervention Platform
- Aging is not uniform: pancreas may be biologically 65 while muscle is 42 in the same person
- Spatial transcriptomics (4.2M cells across 20 ages) shows aging is cell-type-specific in the brain
- Every current intervention is systemic (rapamycin, senolytics, NAD+) — nobody does tissue-specific targeting
- **Specific gap**: no clinical tool that says "your hippocampal interneurons are 15 years older than your chronological age; here is a targeted intervention for those cells"
- **Company unlock**: Precision Longevity — multi-tissue biological age profiling + cell-type-targeted delivery. First app: Alzheimer's prevention 15 years before symptoms. $30-100B

### 2. LINE-1 Retrotransposon Suppression as Aging Therapeutic
- Global DNA methylation declines with age → LINE-1 elements reactivate → cytoplasmic DNA → cGAS-STING → inflammaging
- NRTIs (HIV drugs) can block LINE-1 reverse transcriptase. HIV+ patients on NRTIs have lower Alzheimer's incidence (2019 pharmacoepidemiological signal)
- Zero dedicated companies working on optimized LINE-1 RT inhibitors for aging
- **Company unlock**: LINE-1 RT inhibitor optimized for human aging (not HIV). Indications: Alzheimer's, IPF. $10-50B if NRTI-Alzheimer signal holds in a trial

### 3. Closed-Loop Epigenetic Reprogramming — No Feedback Control
- OSK/OSKM partial reprogramming extends mouse lifespan ~109% in 124-week-old mice
- Safety problem: same factors that reset methylome risk dedifferentiation/teratoma
- Current cyclic approach (2 days ON, 5 days OFF doxycycline) is crude open-loop
- No system measures current epigenetic age of individual cell and titrates OSKM to hit a target biological age without overshooting into pluripotency
- **Wild hypothesis**: EV-miRNA content of supercentenarians (110+) encodes a trickle-charge epigenetic rejuvenation signal. No study has profiled EVs from >50 super-centenarians and tested methylation clock reversal in recipient cells.
- **Company unlock**: Closed-loop reprogramming — analog control system embedded in cell biology. First apps: organ rejuvenation pre-transplant + chronic systemic longevity therapy. $50-200B
